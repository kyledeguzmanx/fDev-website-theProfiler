Actor Profiles

Highlighting the biggest names in television and film that Kyle adores

Now, unfortunately for this section, I do not consume many motion pictures. Actors and actresses are a category of celebrities that I am outrageously blind to. There are many legacy actors and actresses that are houseoldnames, crowd-favorites, and are seen in many movies throughout the years-- and I still do not know who they are.

Further, I am also the type of person who will rewatch entire seasons or films that I love, instead of watching a newly released production. You can say I'm really behind on which actors and actresses are relevant at the moment.

I will say, though, that I do like watching press tours and celebrity interviews in late night talk shows. I'll even watch people that I don't really know. So even if I have never watched the films or shows of some of these celebrities, I can still have an appreciation for them.

Jennifer Lawrence

Jennifer Lawrence is a film actress that's most recognized for The Hunger Games, Joy, American Hustle, Red Sparrow, and Mother. I haven't seen more than half of those movies, but I still appreciate her. She is effortlessly hilarious. Her interviews always drown me in laughter. I especially love her interviews with Stephen Colbert and Ellen Degeneres. Also, when she was doing the press tour for Passangers with her co-star Chris Pratt, I found their friendship incredibly hilarious and heart-warming.

Ryan Reynolds

Ryan Reynolds is a huge online troll that understand internet culture. He makes parodies and troll videos on YouTube either for pure entertainment or for advertisements. Regardless of his motivations, his work is always entertaining to watch. And the videos he produces are full productions with proper editing, proper lighting, and full well-thought-out concepts.

Sarah Paulson

Sarah Paulson is an actress from American Horror Story. She also works heavily with writer Ryan Murphy who produces many hit series such as Ratched, American Horror Story, 911, and The People vs OJ.

I adore Sarah Paulson for many reasons. First and foremost, her acting is beyond phenomenal. She knows how to completely transform herself into the character she's told to play. She convinces you that she's this new character, and she makes you forget the characters she played in the past. She's versatile, similar to her American Horror Story co-stars Lily Rabe, Evan Peters, Jessica Lange, and Angela Bassett

Additionally, she's very charismatic off-screen. She also goes viral every time Ellen Degeneress sneaks up on her and scares her. Those clips of her getting scared get anywhere from 5M to 12M views.

Ashton Kutcher

Ashton Kutcher was on that 70s show. I know he has been in many other motion pictures, but That 70s Show is probably the only work of his I actually watched. I appreciate this guy because he is incredibly intelligent. He's a businessman and investor. He's also a philanthropist. I love watching his speeches and businesses conferences because he always teaches new ideas that I have never explored. When he talks, you can tell he is speaking with passion and knowledge. He genuinely knows the subject he speaks on, and his opinions are always informed. I respect him, what he stands for, and his rationale.
